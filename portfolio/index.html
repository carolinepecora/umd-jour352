About me

Hi! My name is Caroline Pecora and I’m a journalism student at the University of Maryland. I love to write and take pictures, which is highlighted here on my website. Check out my work and find my contact information in the tabs above.

I love going to school in Maryland, but I’m a true Bostonian at heart. Growing up, I always enjoyed reading, writing and just about every other creative outlet, from dancing to drawing. When I’m not working on articles or taking pictures, I might be crocheting a new sweater or getting outside with my dog Jade. I'm also passionate about being an active commnunity member and always ready for an adventure!
