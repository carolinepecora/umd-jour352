Welcome to our website

We are a full service florist and garden center with a selection of antique and vintage furniture as well as accents for the home. We offer plant and garden supplies, as well as beautiful home furnishings to add a touch of flare to living spaces. We pride ourselves on our customer service, and are available to answer any questions you may have. We have a wide variety of antiques and home furnishings that are unique and beautiful. We do not limit our inventory to specific style, which gives us a wider variety of items for you to choose from. We carry a variety of seasonal silk flower stems as well as silk seasonal wreaths and potted plants. We are also available to create custom silk arrangements. We specialize in floral design and arrangements for any occasion such as weddings, birthdays, anniversaries, graduations or sympathy and bereavement flowers. We work with you to make sure your arrangement is exactly how you want it. Please call to inquire about floral delivery!


Whether you are an experienced gardener or just like a few plants in your home, our friendly staff will be able to help you find everything you need to guide you to success. We stock a full supply of seasonal annuals and perennials as well as a wide variety of flowering and evergreen shrubs. We carry an assortment of mulch as well as screened loam. Please call to arrange delivery! Our delivery area for all products includes North Reading, Reading, Wilmington, and Andover. Please call the shop to inquire about delivery to other towns and cities.
